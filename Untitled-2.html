Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Pizza
20 minutes - preparation time ,
10 minutes - cook time ,
30 minutes - Total

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pie shell, baked
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch tbsp
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, mix sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Add water and cook over medium heat until thickened.
  2. Stir a small amount of hot mixture into the egg yolks, then return to the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in butter, lemon juice, and zest.
  4. For the meringue: Beat egg whites and cream of tartar until stiff peaks form. Gradually add sugar and vanilla.
  5. Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 12-15 minutes.
